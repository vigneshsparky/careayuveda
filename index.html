How to Use for Best Results

Follow these simple steps for maximum benefits

1

Apply

Take 2-3 teaspoons of oil and apply to scalp and hair

2

Massage

Gently massage for 5 minutes in circular motions

3

Leave

Leave for 1-2 hours or overnight for deep conditioning

4

Wash

Wash with mild shampoo. Use 2-3 times per week

Caution

  • For external use only
  • Do a patch test before first use
  • Avoid contact with eyes

Storage

  • Keep in a cool, dry place
  • Shake well before use
  • Best before 12 months from packing
